The Problem

The RTO Nightmare, Solved

Millions of Indians relocating across states face a broken vehicle transfer system. Here's what we're fixing.

🗺️

Physical NOC Nightmare

Vehicle owners must physically return to their home state RTO for inspection — impossible for working professionals who've relocated.

💸

Stranded Road Tax

Upfront road tax paid in the old state gets stuck. Refund processes involve heavy bureaucracy and months of waiting.

📅

Missing Deadlines

PUC certificates and insurance expire silently. Fines hit hard. Vehicle owners have no proactive reminder system.

🔢

HSRP Confusion

High Security Registration Plate procurement procedures are unclear, causing compliance delays and legal uncertainty.

🅱️

BH Series Mystery

Most eligible central government employees and defence personnel don't even know they qualify for Bharat Series plates.

📍

PUC Centre Hunting

Finding the nearest authorised PUC testing centre means asking around or searching endlessly — no centralised directory.
