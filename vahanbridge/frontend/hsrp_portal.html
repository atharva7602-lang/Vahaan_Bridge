What is HSRP?

India's High Security
Number Plate System

Mandated by the Supreme Court & MoRTH, HSRP plates come with 7 layers of tamper-proof security — making vehicle identification fraud-proof and traceable.

🔒

Chromium Hologram

A unique hot-stamped chromium-based hologram makes the plate impossible to replicate or tamper with.

🔢

Laser-etched PIN

Each plate has a 10-digit laser-engraved PIN linked to the vehicle's chassis number on the national database.

🛰️

IND Strip + Snap Lock

Tricolour India strip and non-removable snap lock fasteners — once fitted, cannot be removed without destruction.

🎨

Colour-Coded Fuel Sticker

Mandatory colour sticker (blue/orange/green) on windscreen identifies fuel type at a glance for traffic enforcement.

ℹ️ Plate Configuration Rules:
Private vehicles get white plates · Commercial vehicles get yellow plates · EVs get green plates. Fuel sticker colour: Blue for Petrol/CNG, Orange for Diesel, Green for EV/Hybrid. Both are mandatory and non-negotiable under MoRTH rules.
