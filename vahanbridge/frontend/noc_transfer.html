Step 02 / 05

Documents Required
at Home State RTO

Visit your current state's RTO to obtain the NOC (Form 28). Here are all documents you must carry.

📍 Where to go: Your nearest Regional Transport Office (RTO) in your current state. You can also apply online at parivahan.gov.in → Vehicle Related Services → NOC. Carry originals + 2 photocopies of each document.
📄

Form 28 — Application for NOC (In Triplicate)

The official application form for grant of No Objection Certificate for inter-state transfer.

📥 Available at RTO counter or download from parivahan.gov.in
📘

Original RC Book (Registration Certificate)

The vehicle's registration book — original must be submitted. Keep a self-attested photocopy.

📂 Already with you (issued at time of vehicle purchase)
🛡️

Valid Insurance Certificate

Must be currently active and valid. Comprehensive or third-party — both accepted.

🏢 From your insurance company or DigiLocker app
🌿

Valid PUC Certificate (Pollution Under Control)

Must be valid at the time of application. Get a fresh one if expiring soon.

⛽ From any authorized PUC testing center / petrol station
🪪

ID Proof

Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving License (any one).

📱 Available on DigiLocker or UIDAI website
🏠

Address Proof (Current State)

Utility bill, Aadhaar, bank statement, or rent agreement showing current state address.

🏦 Bank statement, electricity bill, or Aadhaar
💰

Tax Clearance Certificate

Proof that all road taxes have been paid in the current state up to date.

🏛️ Obtained from the same RTO — submit before NOC application
📸

Passport-size Photographs

Recent, white-background passport photos of the vehicle owner (3–4 copies recommended).

📷 Any photo studio or instant print shop
🏦

Loan NOC / Form 35 (If Applicable)

If vehicle has an active loan/hypothecation, a clearance letter from your bank or financer is mandatory.

🏦 From your bank/NBFC (HDFC, SBI, ICICI, etc.) — usually takes 3–7 days
🔧

Fitness Certificate (Commercial Vehicles Only)

Valid fitness certificate from an authorized inspection station.

🏛️ From authorized vehicle testing station / RTO
⚠️ Important: The RTO officer will physically inspect your vehicle for Chassis & Engine Number verification. Ensure the vehicle is present at the RTO on the day of application. NOC is valid for 6 months from date of issue.
Step 03 / 05

Upload Home State
Documents

Upload scanned copies or clear photos of your home-state documents. PDF, JPG or PNG — max 5MB each.

💡 Tip: Use your phone camera to take a well-lit, flat photo of each document. Ensure all text is clearly readable. Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG (max 5 MB each).
Step 04 / 05

Documents Required
at New State RTO

After receiving the NOC, you must visit the new state's RTO within 6 months to complete re-registration.

📍 Where to go: The RTO office in your new state (based on your new residential address). Re-registration must be completed within 12 months of relocating.
📄

Original NOC — Form 28

The NOC obtained from your home state RTO. Must be within its 6-month validity period.

📂 Issued by your previous state's RTO
📘

Original RC Book

Your existing registration book. This will be updated or a new one issued by the new state.

📂 Already with you
📝

Form 20 — Application for New Registration

Application for fresh registration in the new state with new registration number.

📥 Available at new state RTO or parivahan.gov.in
🔖

Form 27 — Assignment of New Registration Mark

Application requesting the new state to assign a new registration number to your vehicle.

📥 Available at new state RTO
🛡️

Valid Insurance Certificate

Must be active. Update the registered address on your policy to reflect the new state.

🏢 From your insurer / DigiLocker
🌿

Valid PUC Certificate

A fresh PUC from any authorized center in your new state.

⛽ Any petrol station or authorized PUC center in new state
🏠

ID & Address Proof (New State Address)

Updated Aadhaar, rent agreement, utility bill, or any official document with new state address.

🏦 Electricity board, local authority, or updated Aadhaar
💰

Road Tax Payment Receipt (New State)

You must pay road tax in the new state. After payment, you can claim a pro-rated refund from the old state.

🏛️ Pay at new state RTO or online via state transport portal
📋

Form 29 & 30 (If Ownership Transfer)

If the vehicle ownership is also being transferred to a new person along with state transfer.

📥 Available at any RTO or parivahan.gov.in
📸

Passport-size Photographs

Fresh set of photographs — 3–4 copies recommended.

📷 Any photo studio
⚠️ Road Tax Tip: You'll pay road tax twice initially (old + new state). Keep your old state road tax receipt to claim a pro-rated refund — many states offer this online via their transport portal.

Review &
Submit Application

Verify all your details before final submission. Once submitted, you will receive a reference number for tracking.

document upload status
🕐 What happens next?
1. Visit your Home State RTO with originals for NOC issuance (3–15 working days).
2. Receive NOC (Form 28) — valid for 6 months.
3. Within 6 months, visit New State RTO with NOC + all documents.
4. Pay new state road tax and get new registration number.
5. Apply for old state road tax refund online.
