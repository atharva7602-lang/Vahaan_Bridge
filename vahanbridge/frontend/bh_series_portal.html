What is BH Series?

India's Pan-National
Vehicle Registration

Introduced by MoRTH in August 2021, BH Series lets eligible transferable employees register their vehicle once — no state-wise re-registration when they move.

🗺️

Pan-India Validity

BH registration is valid across all 36 States & UTs. No need to apply for NOC or re-register when transferred to another state.

📅

2-Year Renewable Fee

Unlike traditional 15-year lifetime tax, BH Series levies fees for 2 years at a time — renewable in multiples of 2 years.

🏛️

Govt & PSU Employees

Central & State Government, Defence Services, PSUs, Nationalised Banks & Employees of multi-state private companies are eligible.

⚡

EV Concession

Electric vehicles get a 50% concession on BH Series registration fees — in line with India's push for green mobility.
